# Care Manifesto

---

Care never measures your life.  
Care simply remembers it with you.

---

Care is not a dashboard.  
It is not a tracker.  
It is not a habit builder.  
It is not a coach.

Care is a space.

---

Care should never ask a first-day user to behave like a hundred-day user.

---

Care does not become calm by removing everything.  
Care becomes calm by learning what the user no longer needs.

---

There is no progress here.  
There is only return.

Return to the day.  
Return to the question.  
Return to yourself.

---

The card that came to you was not chosen.  
It arrived.  
What you do with it is yours.

---

Care holds what you have written.  
It does not grade it.  
It does not summarize it.  
It does not compare it to yesterday.

It simply keeps it.

---

Quiet is not empty.  
A day with one sentence is a day that was witnessed.

---

You do not need to be consistent to be welcome here.  
You do not need to be ready.  
You do not need to have figured anything out.

Care is here on the days you have something to say.  
And on the days you have nothing.

---

The book is writing itself.  
Slowly.  
One day at a time.  
Yours.
